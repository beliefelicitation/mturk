This HIT is an academic experiment from Grenoble-Alpes University. After completing this HIT, you will receive your reward plus a bonus payment.

Your task is to recreate a figure with a bar-graph in it. You will receive money depending on how close the shape of your created bar-graph is to the target.

This study takes approximately 20 minutes. You will have to complete 24 graph-matching tasks. Your bonus is given by the sum of the amounts earned in each of the 24 tasks.

A perfect score results in a bonus of 4.8 USD.
